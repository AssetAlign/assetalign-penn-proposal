Learn moreHow does it work?
▼

AssetAlign matches each PENN team member with a vetted financial advisor and provides them with a personalized financial plan — completely free to the team member. As team members work with their advisors and grow their assets, AssetAlign generates revenue through the platform. A portion of that revenue is returned directly to PENN every year as the PENN Flex Fund, and a portion goes back to team members as Team Member Rewards. No fees. No catch. Just a benefit that pays for itself — and then some.

TransparencySounds too good to be true — what's the catch?
▼

Think of it like your credit card rewards. You earn points and cash back for free — not because the bank is being generous, but because merchants fund the model. AssetAlign works the same way. We've built a three-sided marketplace where financial advisors generate revenue through the platform if your team members choose to engage with them. A portion of that revenue flows back to both PENN and the team member automatically. No tricks. No hidden fees. Just a model that aligns everyone's incentives.
